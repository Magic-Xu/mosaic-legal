Why SnapMosaic

A small tool for a common privacy step.

SnapMosaic is built around one job: make sensitive parts of a single image less visible before that image leaves your device.

Three masking effects

Switch between mosaic, blur, and solid block masking depending on how much information you want to hide.

Brush, rectangle, circle

Apply edits with direct brush strokes or quick geometric selections for structured content.

Undo, redo, adjust

Tune strength and size, step backward when needed, and keep the edit flow tight.

Save and share locally

Export results to your device album and use the Android share sheet when you choose to share.
